Web-Based Sales Analytics Dashboard

Created by Hari Om Thakur

This is processed data from raw data

Top Products

  • This bar chart shows the top-selling SKUs (products) based on total sales amount.
  • Helps identify high-revenue items for decision-making.

Monthly Sales Trend

  • Line chart showing how sales change over months.
  • Highlights trends and seasonality.

Gender-wise Sales

  • Pie chart comparing revenue contribution by gender.
  • Helpful for audience targeting and personalization.

Category-wise Performance

  • Shows which categories performed best in sales.
  • Useful for category planning and promotion.

Customer Spend Distribution

  • Histogram of average spending per customer.
  • Helps identify buyer segments and plan offers.
